Team

  • $20,400 sign on bonus (split)

Requirements for Company Drivers

  • Minimum 2 years of CDL A experience
  • Valid CDL A License
  • Minimum Age 25 Years Old
  • Clean MVR
  • Pass drug test
  • No DWI or DUI within last 5 years
  • Clean criminal background check

Owner Operators
