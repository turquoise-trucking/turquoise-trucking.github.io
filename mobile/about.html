About Us

Learn more about Rota Logistics


Rota Logistics is built on over 20 years of experience in the trucking industry. We've provided many companies with efficient and reliable transportation solutions across the contiguous United States. Now, we are leveraging our knowledge to offer third-party logistic services (3PL). In the heart of the Midwest, Rota continues setting the standard for customer satisfaction in 3PL.

Since we established our company, we have been working harder and harder to achieve the best business and the best service in the market.
